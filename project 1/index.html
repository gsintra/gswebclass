If You Give a Mouse a Cookie

by Laura Joffe Numeroff

illustrated by Felicia Bond

If you give a mouse a cookie He is going to ask for a glass of milk. . .

When you give him the milk he'll probably ask you for a straw. . .

When he is finished eating
his cookie and drinking his milk
he will ask for a napkin. . .

then a mirror. . .
then nail scissors. . .
then a broom. . .
then a little bed. . .
then a bedtime story. . .
then paper and crayons. . .
then scotch tape. . .
then milk . . .
And chances are
if he asks for a glass of milk
he is going to want a Cookie to go with it. . .
